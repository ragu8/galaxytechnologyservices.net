GTS Company Overview

 

Global Technology Services (GTS), operating as Gsparks Technology Services Pvt Ltd in India and Galaxy Technology Services Pte Ltd in Singapore, is a leading provider of business continuity planning (BCP), disaster recovery (DR), IT risk and governance, internet of things (IoT), big data, resourcing, and IT cloud infrastructure services in the region. Our executive team has more than 25 years of experience in the IT spectrum across various business domains, which gives us a unique perspective and understanding of the industry. This extensive experience allows us to offer our clients customized solutions that align with their specific business goals and objectives.


Currently, our clients are based in Singapore, India, US, Swiss, New Zealand, and we are in the process of extending our base to Indonesia, Hongkong, Philippines, Malaysia, Vietnam, Taiwan, China, and Thailand. Our services are tailor-made to cater to the specific needs of these regions and are delivered by experienced professionals who have in-depth knowledge of the local market.

We are now equipped to deliver services on the cutting-edge technologies such as quantum computing, artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), and blockchain. These technologies are increasingly being adopted across industries, and we are well-positioned to help our clients to leverage these technologies to drive growth, improve efficiency, and gain a competitive advantage.

Gsparks Technology Services Pvt Ltd in India and Galaxy Technology Services Pte Ltd in Singapore, both under GTS, have a reputation for delivering quality and cost-effective services to their clients. We have a strong presence in the region and have helped many organizations to improve their resilience and agility, reduce costs and improve efficiency, and increase their competitive advantage.

In the future, GTS plans to continue to expand its services and expertise in areas such as IoT, big data, cloud infrastructure, AI, ML and blockchain in the targeted regions. We also plan to increase our focus on emerging markets, where we see significant opportunities for growth.

With our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer service, GTS is well positioned to help organizations navigate the rapidly evolving IT landscape and achieve their business objectives in the targeted regions. With the added advantage of our executive team's experience and expertise and cutting-edge technology offerings we are confident in our ability to provide our clients with the highest level of service and support in the targeted regions.
